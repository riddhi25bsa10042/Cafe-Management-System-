## **Problem Statement**
- Manual cafe operations causing errors and inefficiencies
- No personalized customer experiences or recommendations
- Poor inventory management leading to stockouts/waste
- Lack of data-driven insights for business decisions
- Difficulty scaling operations and menu offerings

## **Scope of the Project**
- Menu management with CRUD operations
- Order processing and billing
- Inventory tracking
- Customer analytics
- Recommendation system
- Sales reporting


## **Target Users**
- Cafe owners/managers (analytics, menu control)
- Cafe staff/cashiers (order processing)
- Business analysts (reporting, insights)

## **High-Level Features**
- **Smart Menu Management** - CRUD operations with stock tracking
- **Intelligent Order Processing** - Automated billing & inventory updates
- **AI Recommendations** - Personalized suggestions using multiple strategies
- **Customer Classification** - VIP/Regular/Occasional/New categories
- **Business Analytics** - Sales reports, revenue tracking, customer insights
- **Sales Simulation** - 7-day forecasting with visualization
- **Inventory Optimization** - Real-time stock monitoring and alerts
- **Admin Dashboard** - System monitoring and performance tracking
